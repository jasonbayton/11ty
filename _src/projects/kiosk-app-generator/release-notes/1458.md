**Features & improvements**

- Added KAG Studio, a guided, chaptered build experience for creating kiosk launcher builds.
- Studio walks through Identity, Canvas, Layout, Apps & Folders, Behavior, Packaging and Review with a live device preview.
- Studio submits into the same KAG build pipeline as the existing form, including APK/source/config downloads and update-code reuse.
