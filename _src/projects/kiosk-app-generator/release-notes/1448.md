**Features & improvements**

- Added a configurable auto-launch grace period. Builds can now set `autostart_grace_seconds` from 1 to 60 seconds, with a default of 10 seconds.
- Exposed the grace period in generated managed configurations so EMMs can adjust auto-launch timing after deployment.
