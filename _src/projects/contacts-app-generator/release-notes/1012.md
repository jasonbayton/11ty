**Features & improvements**

- Renamed individual managed-configuration override keys from dotted names to underscore names for better EMM console compatibility.
- The whole `config_json` shape is unchanged; only flat single-key managed-configuration overrides are affected.

**Fixes**

N/A

**Known issues**

- This is a breaking change for EMMs already pushing dotted keys such as `branding.theme_mode`. Switch to the underscored equivalent, such as `branding_theme_mode`.
